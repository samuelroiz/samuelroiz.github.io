Dixie's New Story

Origin Story

May 1st, 2023: Hoarding dogs rescued!

"In the heart of Bakersfield, a tale of hope unfolded as we embarked on a mission to rescue dogs from a dire hoarding situation. Among the thirteen dogs living in cramped and challenging conditions, a family of six siblings, a charming mix of German Shepherd and Labrador, caught our attention. From this close-knit group, we welcomed three into our care: Dixie, Tucker, and Trudy..."

Read More

Dixie was only four weeks to five weeks old!

"Dixie, embodying a unique blend of shyness and independence, was a delightful enigma. She was a bit more reserved compared to her siblings, often preferring her own company. This independence, however, did not diminish her charm. In the great outdoors, particularly in the lush embrace of the grass, Dixie found her true sanctuary. She would often be seen lounging contentedly, her eyes half-closed in bliss, or delicately prancing around, her every movement a dance with nature. Her love for the open air was evident, as she would spend hours exploring every nook and corner of the garden, her nose skimming the ground, tail wagging in gentle rhythm. In these moments, Dixie's reserved nature melted away, revealing a spirited side, full of life and joy. She was a quiet presence, yet her gentle demeanor and love for the simple pleasures of the outdoors left an indelible mark on our hearts."

Dixie was also sleepy!

"She had an uncanny knack for finding the most unusual spots for her naps, often curling up in places that defied logic but perfectly suited her whimsical nature. Whether it was a cozy corner bathed in sunlight or a soft pile of laundry, Dixie made every spot her own little haven."

Dixie loves outdoors!

"Dixie, our beloved dog, reveled in the lush grass, always eager to frolic outside in the embrace of nature."

Dixie was active!

"In the embrace of the great outdoors, Dixie truly thrived. She delighted in the sensation of the breeze, the whisper of leaves, and the diverse aromas that the wind brought. Observing her frolic and discover, one was inevitably drawn in by her enthusiasm for life. Every jump, every pursuit, every tranquil moment spent under the open sky, depicted a spirit that, despite an initial struggle, discovered happiness in life's simplest joys."

Dixie

"We are delighted to announce that Trudy, our exceptionally well-behaved and intelligent German Shepherd, has found her forever home! Trudy was with us for two months, during which she captured our hearts with her lovely nature and impressive smarts. She was adopted in May, and we couldn't be happier for her. We know she'll bring the same joy and love to her new family as she did to us. Wishing Trudy and her new family many wonderful adventures and cherished moments together!"

Lola

"Great news from our furry friends' corner – Lola, our super active and playful puppy, found her forever home in August! When Lola first came to us, she faced some health challenges, but with dedicated care and love from our team, she blossomed into a healthy and vivacious pup. Her new family, who are avid hikers and beach-goers, are a perfect match for her energetic and adventurous spirit. We're overjoyed knowing Lola is now enjoying life to the fullest, surrounded by love and endless opportunities for fun. Here's to many happy trails and beach days ahead for Lola and her new family!"

Pentunia

We are thrilled to share the heartwarming story of Petunia, a little Shih Tzu who brightened our lives for three wonderful months. From the moment she arrived as a tiny, few-weeks-old puppy, Petunia enchanted us with her loving nature and delightful personality. She had a way of making everyone smile and reminded us so much of our previously adopted Shih Tzu, both in looks and spirit. We are overjoyed to announce that Petunia has found her forever home and is now living a fantastic life, filled with love and joy. It's stories like Petunia's that warm our hearts and remind us of the beauty of fostering.

Heidi and Frank

"We are overjoyed to share the wonderful news about Heidi and Frank, two adorable 15-year-old Chihuahuas who were part of our foster family for over three months. These two were the epitome of love and cuteness, winning the hearts of everyone they met. Their journey with us was filled with affection and sweet moments. We are thrilled to announce that they have found their forever home with a fantastic family, who are a perfect match for them in every way. It's a beautiful sight to see such a special bond formed. We wish Heidi, Frank, and their new family a lifetime of happiness and shared love!"

About

Welcome to Paws for Alohas, a dedicated space for canine compassion and community support. Our mission is to connect loving foster homes with dogs in need, providing a nurturing temporary haven until they find their forever families. We believe every dog deserves a chance at a happy life, and through our website, we aim to make this a reality. Join us in our journey to bring joy and companionship to both dogs and humans alike, one tail wag at a time.
